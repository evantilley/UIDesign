Welcome to the database of mushrooms!
This database contains over 30 entries of various mushrooms!
Curious about a mushroom you found in your backyard? Want to know what that cool glowing mushroom you saw in the forest was? We have everything you need!

Some features that distinguish us from other mushroom databases:
1. Edibility
We provide information on whether a mushroom is edible or not
NOTE: Please always exercise caution when deciding to ingest a mushroom! Just because a mushroom IS edible, doesn't necessarily mean you should consume it! Always follow preparation instructions.
Also Note: We have taken the liberty of classifying hallucinogenic mushrooms as edible, but they are illegal in many regions. Please only consume if advised by a medical professional.

2. Pictures
Our website provides nice pictures of each mushroom so you can easily identify them!

3. Common Names
In addition to the scientific name of each mushroom, we also provide common names. This way, you can use the correct mushroom terminology in day-to-day conversation!
